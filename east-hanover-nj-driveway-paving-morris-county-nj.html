East Hanover NJ Paving

Re-paving your East Hanover NJ driveway or adding a new patio not only improves the look of your home or business but also increases the value of your property. For over 8 years, Asphalt Direct Paving has been providing residential and commercial paving service in East Hanover NJ. Steve Stanko has over 30 years experience and his crew of experienced, courteous and knowledgeable workers complete every job with the pride of quality workmanship.

With our suite of state-of-the-arts well-maintained heavy industrial machinery and a team of experienced workers, we provide a professional, high quality job, on-time, every time and within budget.

Complete paving consists of stone base and hot mix asphalt. Resurface paving consist of removal of loose asphalt, apply leveling course material and final finishing asphalt. In most cases your driveway can be completed in one day.

Our expertise is in Asphalt Driveways that is built to last for many many years. A high quality asphalt driveway is inviting and long lasting.

Add value to your East Hanover NJ property, inquire about our commercial strength asphalt for your East Hanover residential or commercial driveway. We also use Cambridge pavers for your driveway, walkway and retaining walls because they keep their new look for many years to come.
