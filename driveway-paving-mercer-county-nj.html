Residential Paving

Re-paving your driveway or adding a new patio not only improves the look of you home but also increases the value of your property. For over 8 years, Asphalt Direct Paving has been providing residential paving service in New Jersey. Steve Stanko has over 30 years experience and his crew of experienced, courteous and knowledgeable workers complete every job with the pride of quality workmanship.

With our suite of state-of-the-arts well-maintained heavy industrial machinery and a team of experienced workers, we provide professional, high quality job, on-time, every time within budget.

Complete paving consists of stone base and hot mix asphalt. Resurface paving consist of removal of loose asphalt, apply leveling course material and final finishing asphalt. In most cases your driveway can be completed in one day.

Our expertise is in Asphalt Driveways that is built to last for many many years. We simply create a more beautifully paved driveway than the completion. "Driving home to your castle should be an uplifting experience every time every day. High quality asphalt driveway is inviting and long lasting."

Add even more value to your home, inquire about our commercial strength asphalt for your residential driveway. We also use Cambridge pavers for your driveway, walkway and retaining walls because they keep their new look for many years to come.
