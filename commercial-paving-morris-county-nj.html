Commercial Paving

For over 8 years, Asphalt Direct Paving has been providing commercial paving service in New Jersey. Steve Stanko has over 30 years of paving experiance and his crew of experienced, courteous and knowledgeable workers complete every job with the pride of quality workmanship.

Removal of asphalt maybe necessary in some cases. We properly power clean parking lot, apply tac coat and apply leveling course of asphalt in low lying areas.

We machine pave with three inches of commercial grade hot mix asphalt to assure longevity of your parking lot. We carefully do all necessary striping according to the job specifications. In most cases, commercial jobs can be completed in one day.

We also provide seal coating, patching and re-striping of your parking lots.
